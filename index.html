SubSynth

A digital music synthesizer. Created with two classmates in CS510 Computers, Sound and Music. The project was focused on the foundations of subtractive synthesis, one method for creating and manipulating digital (or analog) sound waves It is built with the JUCE framework, a C++ library that was created to make music apps of all kinds, but contains robust functionality for building apps with graphical user interfaces. Project features include: three different oscillators, which alter the quality of the sound(i.e.sine wave, triangle wave, and sawtooth wave patterns); an ADSR envelope for volume control(to control the attack, delay, sustain and release of the tones); options to use an on - screen keyboard or external MIDI keyboard to play the synth, and a visualizer that reacts to the user's actions on the keyboard.;
